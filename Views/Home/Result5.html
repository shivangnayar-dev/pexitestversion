Your Strengths (SWOT analysis)
Careers transcend mere jobs, encompassing behavioural, cognitive, people, and
leadership skills alongside employability traits. Aligning with industry demands
ensures success and fulfilment in chosen paths.
When selecting a career, consider four vital aspects:
1. Behavioural Skills: Handle stress, foster creativity,
embrace learning, uphold ethics, and stay resilient.
2. Cognitive Abilities: Analyse situations, reason logically,
grasp complexity, innovate solutions, and communicate
effectively.
3. People and Leadership Skills: Cultivate confidence,
negotiate skilfully, resolve conflicts, lead teams, embrace
diversity, make wise decisions, and inspire others.
4. Employability Skills: Recognize that employability extends
beyond technical skills, encompassing adaptability and
continuous learning.
Assess yourself across these dimensions to align with
industry demands and maximize your potential for success
and fulfilment.
