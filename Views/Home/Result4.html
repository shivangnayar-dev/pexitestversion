Motivation of work
Different people are motivated by different things. Some want to be liked and
accepted by others, so they work hard to fit in and be recognized. Others value
their independence and creativity, preferring to do things their own way and
express themselves freely. Then there are those who strive for excellence in
what they do, aiming to make a positive impact and be the best they can be.
Finally, some are driven by the desire for power and wealth, wanting to achieve
success and control their own destiny. Figuring out what motivates you can
help you set goals and find satisfaction in your work and life.
