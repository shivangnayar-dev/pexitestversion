Your Temperament
"Success is not the key to happiness. Happiness is the key to success.
If you love what you are doing, you will be successful." - Albert
Schweitzer
Temperament is like the blueprint of who we are from the moment we're born. It shapes
how we react to things and what we're naturally drawn to. Think of it as the foundation of
your personality, influenced by both your genes and early experiences. But as we go
through life, things like how we're raised, the culture we're part of, and the people we're
around, all play a part in shaping and refining our temperament. Even though it changes
over time, those core traits stick with us, guiding how we see the world and how we handle
different situations. Understanding our temperament helps us understand ourselves
better and grow as individuals.
You present a tough persona while your temperament is
caring and a gentle approach to humanity. This temperament
suits careers that require high energy but also a humane
approach and imagination. You may try jobs that are closer
to your home town, so that it allows you to serve humanity
with being close to the family would be highly satisfying for
you.
